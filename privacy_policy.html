GrowDaily Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

This policy explains what information GrowDaily ("the app", "we") collects, how it's used, and the choices you have. GrowDaily is an Islamic habit-tracking app. We built it around a simple principle: no ads, and we never sell your data.

Information We Collect

Account information. When you create an account, we collect the email address and password you provide (handled by Firebase Authentication, a Google service). You can also use the app as a guest without creating an account; in that case no account information is collected at all.

Habit and progress data. The habits you track, your daily completions, streaks, XP, gold, focus plans, and similar in-app progress are stored under your account in Cloud Firestore (also a Google/Firebase service) so your data syncs across your devices. A copy is also cached locally on your device for offline use.

Purchase information. If you subscribe to or purchase GrowDaily Premium, the purchase is processed entirely by Apple's App Store. We use RevenueCat to confirm your subscription status with Apple. GrowDaily and RevenueCat never see or store your card details — only Apple does.

Prayer-time location (optional). If you enable prayer-time reminders, you can type in your city name. That city name is sent to Open-Meteo, a free third-party geocoding service, solely to resolve it into coordinates used for calculating local prayer times. We do not access your device's GPS or precise location, and this feature is entirely optional.

Voice notes (optional). If you record a voice note on a task, the recording is saved only on your own device. It is never uploaded to our servers or anywhere else.

Rooms (group challenges). If you join or create a Room to do a habit challenge with friends, your display name and daily progress on the shared habit(s) are visible to other participants in that same Room.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information above to operate the app's core features: syncing your habits and progress across devices, unlocking Premium after a verified purchase, scheduling the reminders you've asked for, and showing your progress to other members of a Room you've joined. We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell or rent your information to anyone.

Third-Party Services

GrowDaily relies on the following third-party services to operate, each of which processes a limited slice of your data as described above:

  • Firebase (Google) — account authentication and app data storage.
  • Apple App Store — processes all payments; GrowDaily never handles your payment details directly.
  • RevenueCat — confirms subscription/purchase status with Apple on our behalf.
  • Open-Meteo — resolves a typed city name into coordinates for prayer-time calculation, if you use that feature.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using Firebase's infrastructure, which applies industry-standard security practices. While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission is ever 100% secure.

Your Choices and Rights

You can edit or remove individual habits and data at any time from within the app. If you'd like to permanently delete your account, go to Profile → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and all associated data from our systems and cannot be undone.

Children's Privacy

GrowDaily is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
